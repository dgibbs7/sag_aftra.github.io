Why did actors want to unionize? from Film Actors Organize: Union Formation Efforts in America, 1912-1937 by Kerry Seagrave, pg 169

  • Inconsistent and Low Paying Work
  • Working long hours, sometimes having a seven day work week
  • Actors were informed if they had to work again the following day five minutes before they left the studio
  • Actors had to pay for their own wardrobe
  • No minimum rest period between being called in to work
  • Actors would not get paid until they arrived on location, and did not take into account their transportation costs whether through public transit or car
